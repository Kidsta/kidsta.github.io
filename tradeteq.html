2023 ‚Äì 2024

The Challenge

At Tradeteq, my role shifted depending on where design support was needed most. The main challenge was that the company had multiple internal and client-facing platforms, all built at different times with little long-term planning. The result was a fragmented experience with no unifying design language.

  • Lack of consistency: Each platform had its own look and feel, making the ecosystem disjointed.
  • No design system: Components were duplicated, inconsistent, or unused across products.
  • Ad-hoc workflows: New components were often created in isolation without clear approval or governance.
  • My role: Audited all existing components and products, consolidated them into a unified design system, and introduced a process for approving and maintaining new components. I also supported product teams with day-to-day design needs for niche client features.
